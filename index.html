Who We Are

About Our Trust

Est. in Varanasi

Vishwa Kashi Ganges Charitable Trust is a charitable initiative based in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, established with the purpose of serving humanity through Annadan Seva (food donation).

Every day, thousands of devotees travel to Kashi to seek blessings of Mahadev. Many pilgrims come from different states with limited financial support. At the same time, several local families in Varanasi struggle to afford basic meals.

Our trust works to ensure that no devotee and no needy person sleeps hungry in the holy land of Kashi.

We believe that serving food is one of the highest forms of service. Through community support and donations, we organize free meal distribution for:

  • ✦ Pilgrims visiting Kashi
  • ✦ Poor and underprivileged individuals
  • ✦ Daily wage workers in need
  • ✦ Elderly people without support

Our work is rooted in the values of Seva (service), Dharma (righteousness), and Sanskar (cultural values).

What We Do

Annadan Seva

We organize regular free meal distribution programs in Varanasi for devotees visiting Kashi Vishwanath, travelers who cannot afford meals, and poor and homeless individuals.

Food is prepared hygienically and distributed with respect and dignity.

Many pilgrims travel long distances for darshan. We aim to support them with basic food assistance during their spiritual journey in the holy city of Kashi.

  • Birthdays & Anniversaries
  • Religious Occasions
  • Special Remembrance Days

Donors can contribute funds that directly support food preparation and distribution.

Why Annadan in Kashi?

Kashi is one of the most sacred cities in India. Devotees visit throughout the year seeking spiritual blessings. Providing food in this holy land is considered an act of great virtue.

"Annadan is not just charity. It is service to humanity and devotion to Mahadev."
